Our Story

A Bakery Born from Passion

Golden Crust Bakery was founded in 2010 by Priya and Rajan Mehta in the heart of Surat. What started as a small home kitchen delivering fresh sourdough to neighbors has grown into one of Surat's most beloved neighborhood bakeries.

We believe that good bread takes time — no shortcuts, no additives. Every loaf is crafted by hand using traditional techniques passed down through Priya's family.
